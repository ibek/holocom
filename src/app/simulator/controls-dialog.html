Controls

Zoom

Use mouse scroll wheel mouse to zoom in or out.

Move Ship Up or Down

Have a selected ship(s) and use the mouse scroll wheel mouse to move the ship up or down.

2D Rotation

Have a selected ship(s). Mouse Right Click will rotate the ship(s) towards the target.

3D Rotation

Have a selected ship(s). Hold CTRL key during mouse Right Click which will rotate the ship(s) towards the target.

Switch Ship to Enemy or Ally

Have a selected ship(s) and click on invert_colors to switch the selected ship to enemy or ally.

Show Ship Info

Have a selected single ship. Click on the label with the ship's name.
