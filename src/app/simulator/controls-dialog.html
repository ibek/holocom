Controls

Though it's possible to move around the scene on your mobile phone or tablet, it's recommended to use your PC for creating the tactical plans.

Camera Orientation

zoom_in Zoom View

Use mouse scroll wheel mouse or touch pinch to zoom in or out.

camera_roll Rotate View

Use mouse Right Click or virtual joystick to rotate the scene view.

camera Lock View to a Ship

Have a selected ship and click on camera to lock the camera view to the selected ship. To unlock the view, click to the same button camera again.

Scene Editing

Have a selected ship(s) to do any of the actions below.

swap_vertMove Ship Up or Down

Hold SHIFT key + Mouse Scroll Wheel mouse to move the ship up or down.

rotate_90_degrees_ccw 2D Ship Rotation

Hold SHIFT key + Mouse Right Click will rotate the ship(s) towards the target. Alternatively press C or V to rotate the ship(s) to the left or right. To reset the ship(s)' rotation, press X.

3d_rotation 3D Ship Rotation

Hold CTRL key + Mouse Right Click will rotate the ship(s) towards the target.

invert_colorsSwitch Ship to Enemy or Ally

Click on invert_colors or press F to switch the selected ship to enemy or ally.

info_outlineShow Ship Info

Click on the label with the ship's name; for example "F7C Hornet".

Animation Recording

Have a selected ship(s) and click on fiber_manual_record to enable the recording. The length of the scene animation can be set in Settings.

add_locationAdd ship route

Mouse Right Click to add new points to the ship's route. Maximum limit is 5 points.

edit_locationEdit ship route

To move one of the points of the route, use Mouse Left Button. To move the point up or down, get the mouse cursor over the point and hold SHIFT key + Mouse Scroll Wheel mouse. To delete the point, use Mouse Right Button.

visibility_offHide ship on its route

Hold CTRL key + Mouse Scroll Wheel over the point mouse to change the ship's visibility in that particular point's position. The point will change its color to Black. At least one point will always stay visible.

fast_forwardManually change the current time of animation

Mouse Left Click on the timeline. Alternatively, hold key 2 to move animation forward or hold key 1 to move animation backward.
