Controls

Zoom

Use mouse scroll wheel mouse to zoom in or out.

Move Ship Up or Down

Select a ship and use the mouse scroll wheel mouse to move the ship up or down.

3D Rotation

Hold CTRL key, then choose the target with the mouse cursor and mouse Right Click will rotate the ship(s) towards the target.

Switch Ship to Enemy or Ally

Select a ship and click on invert_colors to switch the selected ship to enemy or ally.
