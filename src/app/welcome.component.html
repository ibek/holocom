WELCOME CITIZEN

UEE Tactical Communications Interface is a tool and fan project for tactical planning in Star Citizen, the game from CIG.

1. Add Ships to Inventory

  • Ships are divided into 3 categories: Small, Medium, and Large.
  • Every category is visualized in a separate row
  • Once your list of ships in the Inventory is ready, click on the Simulate button.

2. Simulate and Make a Tactical Plan

  • See Controls to learn camera orientation, scene editing, and animation recording.
  • Play your tactical plan animation.

3. Share the Tactical Plan with your friends

Copy & Paste the URL. Be aware that a tactical plan which has not been viewed for more than 2 months will be removed.
